I Am the author of "Diary of A Wimpy Kid"

Hi!

Jeff Kinney was born on Feb 19, 1971. He lived in Fort Washington, Maryland. Kinney graduated school in 1989. Kinney had an older brother and a younger brother. As you all know Kinney is famous for his book series "Diary of a Wimpy Kid"

The book became an instant hit, with the online version receiving about 20 million views as of 2007. When many online readers requested a printed version, Kinney agreed, and in April 2007, Diary of a Wimpy Kid was published.[4] To date, eighteen Wimpy Kid books have been released, including a movie diary and a Do-It-Yourself Book, and even a biography that Gregory's best friend wrote.[6] In April 2009, TIME named Kinney one of The World's Most Influential People.[7] In 2010, 2011, 2012, 2014, 2015, and 2016 the series won a Nickelodeon Kids Choice Award for Favorite Book.
